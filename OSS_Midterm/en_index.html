Sushi & BBQ

We are Jeju sushi with the hidden charm of Jeju Island. Our restaurant was inspired by the beautiful nature and sea of Jeju Island.

Made from fresh seafood harvested directly from the blue sea of Jeju Island, our dishes express the natural taste of the ingredients. Our menu changes with the seasons and we try to capture the rich taste of Jeju Island as much as possible for each season.

We provide a warm atmosphere and friendly service so that you can enjoy your meal comfortably. At our restaurant where you can feel the beauty of Jeju Island, enjoy a special meal away from your daily life.
